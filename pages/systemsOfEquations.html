Systems of Linear Equations

Systems of Linear equations are when you have two or more linear equations that are “working together” (or working against you, depending on how hard the problem turns out). Your objective is to solve for x and y.

Possible Solutions

  1. DNE - the sytem is ‘parellel’ to each other aka there is no value(s) for x and y that satifies all the equations in the system
  2. Infinte many solutions - the equations are equivalent
  3. There is a value for x and y =)

There are 3 ways to solve:

  1. Substitution
  2. Elimination
  3. Graphing

Solve Using Substitution

Equation 1: 2x = y
Equation 2: 3x + 6 = y

Sub equation 1 into equation

3x + 6 = 2x

Solve

3x-2x = 6
x = 6

Solve for y by plugging into equation one

2(6) = y
12 = y
