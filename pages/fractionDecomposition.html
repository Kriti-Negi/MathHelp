Partial Fraction Decomposition

Basically taking a polynomial-fraction and decomposing it into its polynomial factors

How to Solve:

  1. Factor the denominator of your original equation into rational and irreducible bits
  2. Write out the factors as fractions added up. You don't know the numerators of the fractions yet, so you replace those with variables such as A, B and C.
  3. Solve for the variables based on the power of 'x'
  4. Yeah... that's it.
